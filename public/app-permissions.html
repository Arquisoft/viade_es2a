Application Permissions

To use Viade, you must grant Read, Write, Append, and Control permissions to allow the app to create and read routes files in your Pod.

To manage your Trusted Applications

  1. Log into your Pod.
  2. Display your Preferences.
  3. In the Manage your trusted applications table, against the Application URL of the Generator application you are using:
    1. Select Read, Write, Append, and Control Access Modes. (i.e., all checkboxes are selected).
    2. Click "Update" under Actions.
You should now be able to use the app.
